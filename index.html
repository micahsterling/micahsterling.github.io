About me

Tech Sales Rep turned Software Engineer. I am a hard working, fast learning, and diligent individual that will rarely take no for an answer. There is always a solution to every problem and it is my goal to figure out what that is.

I started getting interested in coding a few years back and started to teach myself here and there with Codecademy, like many people do. At the time I was not really thinking of making a career change and it fell by the wayside. In the meantime, my younger brother was getting his undergraduate degree in Computer Information Systems and then went on to get a Masters. Talking with him about what he was doing, first in his major and then his new job, sparked my interest again. When it came time for me to make a career change, I knew the direction I should go. I went back to Codeacademy and eventually found myself at The Odin Project and started to really enjoy the craft.

After making a fatal mistake in the command line I deleted all of my precious files and folders. That prompted days and days of questioning if I was actually cut out to do this, but I eventually picked myself up, dusted myself off, and knew that I wanted to make a career out of coding. I enjoy problem solving, figuring out how things tick, taking them apart and making them better. It brings me great joy to solve a problem that has been stumping me for a while so I was not going to let this setback stop me.

My next step was a bootcamp where I learned how to have the right mindset and think like a coder on top of the fundamentals and how to teach myself new technologies. I am excited for both what I can learn next and how I can have an impact on the world through code. I dont like to define myself by the work I have done but by the work that I want to do. Skills can be taught personality and attitude not so much
