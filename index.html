About me

Tech Sales Rep turned Software Engineer. I am a hard-working, fast learning, and diligent individual. If there is a solution to a problem it is my goal to figure out what that solution is.

My experience in tech sales has given me a unique perspective on the tech world. In previous roles, I had to communicate with people that had varying levels of technical knowledge, which meant I needed to be able to understand on a technical level what the engineers needed and then explain to non-technical parties why they needed it.

Since graduating from a coding boot camp, I have been working on various projects to continue to expand my fluency in coding languages such as: solving algorithm problems using sites like Exercism and Leetcode; working with interactive tutorials like Scrimba to strengthen my understanding of key concepts; and working on various coding projects detailed below.
